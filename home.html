Menses / Periods

from 01/05/2021 to 04/05/2021

The period of vaginal discharge. The innermost layer of uterus(Endometrium) is shed along with blood as vaginal discharge.Sexual Intercourse should be avoided in this phase due to pain in lower abdomen. There are mood changes and depression during this period. Pain in abdomen, cramps etc. can also be felt. Proper sleep is recommended during this period.

Pre-Ovulatory Safe Period

from 05/05/2021 to 07/05/2021

Time period before ovulation. It is the time period when ovulation doesn't occur and cervical mucosa is dry (i.e. without fluid). If during this period, you notice any kind of cervical mucus (You can check by inserting two finger from vagina till cervix and can feel the mucus), Try to avoid having intercourse until the post-ovulatory safe period.

Fertile Period

from 08/05/2021 to 20/05/2021

Fertile period can also be termed as 'Unsafe Period' as chances of conception are very high during this time. Sexual Intercourse should be avoided during this period to avoid pregnancy and should be performed during this period for getting pregnant. This period Extends from 5 days before ovulation (As sperms can be functional upto 5 days inside female body) and about 2 to 3 days after ovulation. But as there is an average variation of 2 days in the day of ovulation, So this period extends from 7 days before the expected date of ovulation to about 5 days after the expected date of ovulation(but if you feel that ovulation happened then this period will only extends until 3 days after ovulation).

Post-Ovulatory Safe Period

from 21/05/2021 to 28/05/2021

This period starts from about 3 days after ovulation or 3 days after the mucus in the cervix reaches its peak(You can check it by inserting two fingers through vagina until cervix and check if mucus is there or not)Click here to know more about cervical mucus This is the safest period in the entire cycle as the chances of pregnancy are almost zero during this period. This period exists till the beginning of next menstrual bleeding. Misinterpretation of the beginning of this period can lead to pregnancy.
